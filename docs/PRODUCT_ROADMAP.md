# Product Roadmap

## Overview

The MwalaPay product roadmap outlines the strategic development plan for the platform. The roadmap highlights upcoming features, infrastructure improvements, and ecosystem expansion that will transform MwalaPay into a full digital financial platform.

The goal is to gradually evolve the platform from a **digital wallet solution** into a **complete financial ecosystem**.

---

# Phase 1 – Core Payment Infrastructure

Focus: Build the foundational financial platform.

Key features:

- User registration and authentication
- Wallet management
- Peer-to-peer transfers
- Deposit and withdrawal functionality
- Transaction history
- Immutable ledger system
- Basic KYC verification
- Mobile-friendly interface

Outcome:

A reliable digital wallet platform that allows users to send, receive, and store money securely.

# Phase 2 – Financial Integrations

Focus: Connect the platform to external financial services.

Key features:

- Mobile money integrations
- Bank account linking
- Cross-platform fund transfers
- Automated reconciliation
- Secure payment APIs

Outcome:

Users can seamlessly move funds between MwalaPay and external financial systems.

# Phase 3 – Online Payments

Focus: Enable online commerce and global payments.

Key features:

- Virtual payment cards
- Merchant payment tools
- Online checkout system
- International payment support
- Cross-border transactions

Potential integration partners may include global payment networks such as Mastercard and PayPal.

Outcome:

Users gain the ability to pay for international services and participate in global digital commerce.

# Phase 4 – Financial Services Expansion

Focus: Expand into broader financial services.

New features may include:

- Savings accounts
- Micro-loans
- Investment tools
- Crypto asset management
- Automated budgeting tools

Outcome:

MwalaPay becomes a multi-service financial platform.

# Phase 5 – Financial Ecosystem

Focus: Build a financial ecosystem for businesses and developers.

Key features:

- Public APIs for developers
- Merchant payment platforms
- Financial analytics dashboards
- Payment gateway services
- Marketplace integrations

Outcome:

MwalaPay becomes a central financial infrastructure for digital businesses in Malawi.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision is to establish MwalaPay as a trusted financial platform capable of supporting:

- national digital payments
- cross-border financial services
- digital commerce
- financial inclusion

This vision aligns with the broader growth of the digital economy and expanding financial accessibility.
